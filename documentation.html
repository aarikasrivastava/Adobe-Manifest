Adobe Manifest User Guide


Refer to this guide to understand the working of Adobe Manifest. Follow the steps carefully.


Step 1 (Change browser settings):


  • Go to ‘browser’-> ‘Settings’ ->’Downloads’-> Turn on ‘ask where to save before downloading’. This step will enable you to download the report in the folder of your choice.



    • Step 2 (Format for excel upload):

  • Uploaded excel should have the given format: [Customer name, Transaction number, Number of annual Transactions]




  • First row of excel should have headings as mentioned. Second Row of the excel sheet must have respective values filled.




    • Step 3 (Working of Adobe Manifest portal):





  • Login here by entering the correct username and password (Ask your manager for the credentials) and click on Login button.




  • After login you will be redirected to the ‘Home’ page. Here you can click on either ‘Single customer’ entry or ‘Multiple customer entry’ according to your needs (explained below).
  • On the top right corner, you can click logout to go back to login screen.




    • For ‘Multiple Customer Entries’:

  • Choose File -> Select Excel file with customer information with correct format (refer to Step 2) -> Submit




  • You will be prompted to select the desired folder to download the value realization report (If this prompt is not coming, refer to Step 1).




  • The final report will be downloaded with date and time for future references.

    • For ‘Single Customer Entr’:

  • Click on ‘Single customer entry’ radio button. You will be redirected to the form displayed below:




  • Add customer information/ edit the assumptions by clicking on the fields.




  • Select required currency conversion and click ‘Submit Form’.




  • Select location for file download and click ‘save’.
